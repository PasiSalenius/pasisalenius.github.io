Teletext

Teletext Watch

App Store link

Read teletext right on your Apple Watch.

Navigate pages by swiping up and down on the screen. Move between subpages by swiping sideways. A double tap toggles the optional zoom mode, which gives you a closer look at the page contents. The digital crown can be used to quickly jump to any page number. Force touching the display shows other options, like access to the page catalogue.

The app remembers the last viewed page, and refreshes it upon display, so you always have quick access to your information.

At the moment only teletext news from Yle and MTV are supported.
